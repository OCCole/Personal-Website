Carolina Wren:

LocationCarolina Wrens live around the eastern side of the United States. While they can be found in other places, you can easily find them in the Carolinas.
Appearance:Carolina Wrens are short brown birds, and are smaller than Cardinals.Click Here to View Images of Carolina Wrens on Google
Bird Song: The Carolina Wren's typical song is esily identifiable, once you know what you are listening for. More likely than not, you have heard one before. Although the Carolina Wren can be identified by it's song, the bird makes a variety of other noises-ranging from the sound of a simple "tweet" to the sound of a squeaky metal gate.
Fun Fact: Carolina Wrens are the state bird of South Carolina. If you get the chance to observe them you might see them bounce up and down while quickly switching directions, as if they are doing squats.
